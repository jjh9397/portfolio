Biography

Hi! I'm a fourth year student studying computer science and premed at Northwestern. I grew up in the suburbs of Chicago and Shanghai, and now live in Evanston. At Northwestern, my major in computer science and interest in medicine led to exciting research opportunities such as the Smart PPE project with the Ka Moamoa lab. Beyond academics, I was able to participate as a volunteer peer mentor for the YW Tech Lab web development class for underserved members of the community.


In my free time, I enjoy drawing and painting. Some of my artworks and studies are shown on the home page gallery. I also enjoy fencing, and fence epee and serve as the treasurer for the Northwestern fencing club.
