Biography

Hi! I'm a fourth year student studying computer science and premed at Northwestern. I grew up in the suburbs of Chicago and Shanghai, and now live in Evanston. At Northwestern, my major in computer science and interest in medicine led to exciting research opportunities such as the Smart PPE project with the Ka Moamoa lab, where I currently work as a research assistant on projects involving low power embedded systems and their applications towards healthcare.


Beyond academics, I am a volunteer peer mentor for the YW Tech Lab web development class for underserved members of the community, and am also a state licensed Basic Emergency Medical Technician. In my free time, I enjoy drawing and painting, and I've shown some of my artworks and studies in my home page gallery. I also enjoy fencing epee, and serve as the treasurer for the Northwestern fencing club.
