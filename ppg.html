Pulse Plethysmography Processing

In this ongoing research project, I used Python in order to process PPG signals into vital information such as heart rate, respiration rate, oxygen saturation, and blood pressure as part of a signal processing pipeline for an embedded device. In the below figures, I recreate an IMS peak picking algorithm with modifications to account for our noisier signal from a novel sensor placement. From the line segments generate, I seperate out different components of the signal (Amplitude Modulation, Baseline Wander, and Frequency Modulation) to extract a respiratory signal.
